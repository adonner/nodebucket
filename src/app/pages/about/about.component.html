Twin Lab Works was founded in 2018 by a young, up-and-coming developer named Adam Donner while he was a student at Bellevue University. What started as a class project, has grown rapidly to the company that it is today. Adam, along with a skilled group of developers at Twin Lab Works, are developing the future that they want to live and work in.

At Twin Lab Works we have one goal in mind, to create beautiful web applications that are both easy and intuitive. We create the web tools that promote a more organized, more fulfilling way to work and live. Not only do we have a a suite of software ready to deploy in any business environment, we also develop custom software that can be built specifically for our customer’s business needs.

One example of our suite of ready to deploy software is nodebucket. nodebucket is an easy to use task management application that keeps track of all of your tasks, projects, and goals in one simple, easy to use application. With a familiar drag and drop interface and easy to understand buttons, nodebucket really is an application that any user can login and begin enjoying right away!

Features of nodebucket:

  • nodebucket gives its users an easy to see picture of everything that is on their ToDo list so they won’t lose track of any important task
  • nodebucket allows users to organize their tasks so they always know what they need to be focused on
  • nodebucket shows what tasks have been completed to help show what has been accomplished
  • Nodebucket can be used on any device-phone, tablet, or computer

nodebucket was developed using the MEAN stack, MongoDB, Express, Angular, and Node.js.
